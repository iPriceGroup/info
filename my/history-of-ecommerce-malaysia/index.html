Methodology

  1. The most popular eCommerce was ranked with the highest average search interest recorded in the specified year on Google Trends
  2. The top mobile applications were ranked based on the highest average rank in the specified year on App Annie
  3. All screenshots were obtained from the Wayback Machine and Google Images
  4. In 2015, 65daigou (now rebranded as Ezbuy) was ranked out of the top 10 list
