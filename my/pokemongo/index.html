How Big is the Pokémon Go phenomenon in Malaysia?

On the 6th of July 2016, Pokémon Go was released with many eager fans waiting in the tall green grasses. Since then, the app has garnered millions of users worldwide, even surpassing Twitter for daily active users. While the app has yet to make its debut in Malaysia, that hasn’t stopped it from sparking major interest amongst the locals. To see just how big this phenomenon is, we have made some data comparisons with the volume of online searches related to other major local and international events of recent history.

GST, short for Goods and Services Tax, sparked interest with the implementation of a 6% tax. Many of the locals thought it was an April Fool’s Joke, as it was implemented on the 1st of April, but it was certainly not a joke.

While the franchise is usually famous for its insane car scenes and Diesel’s manliness, it was the movie’s tribute to one of its leading actors, Paul Walker, who had lost his life in a car accident in 2013, that sparked the highest interest.

A TV series adaptation of a book, written by Nur Ain Al-Abbas, about a cynical girl who receives a proposal from a secret admirer. It was released on the 30th of March 2015 with Mia Ahmad and Syarul Ridzwan as the show’s leads.

Flappy Bird

Fab 2014 15

Despite its release in 2013, Dong Nguyen’s brainchild only gained popularity in January 2014 before being removed a month later for being too addictive. As of January 2014, it was the most downloadable free game in the iOS app store.

This TV favourite, hosted by Dato’ AC Mizal, features comedians from Malaysia, Singapore and Indonesia to perform skits onstage in front of a live audience. By the end of each season, the King of Comedy is crowned.

Badminton

Lee Chong Wei, a notable member of the Malaysian badminton team, had an impressive run in the Summer Olympics 2012. Even though he had lost the finals to Lin Dan, Malaysia proud so the day after was declared a public holiday!

iPhone

The search interest for the iPhone peaked in October 2011 with the release of the iPhone 4s, the first in the iPhone series to sport an ‘s’. The passing of Steve Jobs in the same month also played a big part in the peak of interest.

Obama

Barack Obama made American history by being the first African American U.S. president. His impressive campaign and election caught the attention of not only the American public but worldwide as well.
