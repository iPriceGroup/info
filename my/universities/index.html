1 Tech

Calling all tech nerds and geeks, come and help build new features and improve our iprice online store. Showcase your knowledge of coding and create a friendly one stop shopping destination for the growing online shopping community.

  • A Bachelor's Degree/Diploma in Computer Science, Information Technology or a related subject

  • Experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, client-side libraries like jQuery, jQuery UI, Bootstrap or Foundation
