How Big is the Pokémon Go phenomenon in Southeast Asia?

On the 6th July 2016, Pokémon GO was released with many eager fans waiting to have a go at it (no pun intended). Since then, the app has garnered millions of users worldwide, surpassing the daily active Twitter users. While the app has yet to make its debut in Southeast Asia, we are already buzzing with excitement for its arrival. To see how big this phenomenon is to date, iprice has made an analysis, comparing volume of online searches in relation to other recent major local and international topics.
